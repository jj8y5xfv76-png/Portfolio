Workflow — How I Edit

From discovery to delivery — a clear, collaborative process built for efficiency and consistency.

1. Discovery

Define the goal, audience, and expectations of the project. Establish key deliverables and timeline to set a strong foundation before production begins.

2. Asset Gathering

All footage, slides, demos, bumpers, music, and brand guidelines are collected and organized for editing, ensuring a smooth and efficient workflow.

3. Assembly

The edit is constructed — pacing, flow, and structure are refined to reach the first full draft.

4. Feedback & Refinement

The draft is shared with stakeholders for review and feedback, leading to revisions that align with the creative and business goals.

5. Delivery

The final version is exported, reviewed, and delivered to the client. Additional assets such as captions or alternate formats are provided as needed.

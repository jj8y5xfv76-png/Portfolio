Workflow — How I Edit

My process spans creative exploration and structured, standards-driven delivery.

Exploration → Finding the Cut

I love the play phase — testing rhythms, trying alt music cues, and discovering the emotional spine. I build a rough sequence quickly, mark story beats, and keep time-stamped notes so ideas are traceable.

Structure → Brand-Safe Delivery

When precision matters, I switch into standards mode: consistent templates, captioning, and repeatable exports. Feedback is collected in Frame.io with timestamped comments for accountability.
